Our History

Sedgefield Classic Cars has become one of the most popular tourist attractions on the Garden Route. We are situated between George and Knysna and our depot is easily located with a variety of 50 to 60 classic cars at all times, including veteran, vintage, post vintage to post 1945 vehicles. We also have 4X4's Jeeps, motorcycles, Phaetons, roadsters, coupes, sedans and pick-ups, to sports cars, moderns and commercial vehicles. We will assist in finding your dream car.
