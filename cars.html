Our History

Sedgefield Classic Cars has become one of the most popular tourist attractions on the Garden Route. We also have 4X4's Jeeps, motorcycles, Phaetons, roadsters, coupes, sedans and pick-ups, to sports cars, moderns and commercial vehicles. We will assist in finding your dream car.
